Spice-Rubbed Chicken Lettuce Cups





Ingredients

1/4 cup olive oil

1 Tbsp. paprika

1 Tbsp. ground cumin

1 Tbsp. light brown sugar

2 tsp. dried oregano

2 tsp. onion powder

1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper

Kosher salt and pepper

3 8-ounce boneless, skinless chicken breasts (11/2 pounds)

1/2 cup sour cream

1 tsp. lime zest plus 1 to 2 tablespoons juice
